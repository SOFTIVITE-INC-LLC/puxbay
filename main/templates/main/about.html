We empower retailers to grow.

At Puxbay, we believe that powerful technology shouldn't be reserved for the giants. We build accessible, intuitive tools that help small and medium businesses compete and thrive.

Our Story

It started in 2018 with a simple observation: local shop owners were spending more time battling their outdated cash registers than helping their customers. The available software was clunky, expensive, and tied to specific hardware.

We set out to change that. We wanted to build a POS system that ran in the cloud, worked on any device, and was actually a joy to use.

Today, Puxbay supports thousands of retailers across the globe. From cozy coffee shops to bustling clothing boutiques, our platform processes millions of transactions every year, giving owners the freedom to run their business from anywhere.
