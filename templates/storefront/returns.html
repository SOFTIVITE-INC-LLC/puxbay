Returns & Exchanges

Your satisfaction is our priority

Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we offer returns and exchanges within a reasonable timeframe.

Eligibility Requirements

  • check_circle Items must be in original condition with tags attached
  • check_circle Original receipt or order confirmation required
  • check_circle Items must be unused and in resalable condition
  • check_circle Return within 14 days of purchase

How to Return

1

Contact Us

Reach out to our support team with your order number and reason for return

2

Get Approval

We'll review your request and provide return authorization if eligible

3

Return Items

Bring items to the store with your receipt and authorization

4

Get Refund

Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days

Exchanges

If you'd like to exchange an item for a different size, color, or product, we're happy to help! Exchanges follow the same eligibility requirements as returns.

Simply bring the item to our store along with your receipt, and our team will assist you in finding the perfect replacement.

Non-Returnable Items

For health and safety reasons, the following items cannot be returned:

  • Opened or used personal care items
  • Perishable goods
  • Final sale or clearance items
  • Gift cards
